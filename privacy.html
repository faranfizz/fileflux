Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Files You Upload

Files you upload to FluxFiles are processed solely for the purpose of conversion. All uploaded files are automatically deleted from our servers within 1 hour of conversion. We do not store, read, share, or sell your files or their contents under any circumstances.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal anonymous usage data such as page views and tool usage frequency to improve our service. We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

3. Cookies

We use cookies only for essential site functionality and anonymous analytics. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes beyond standard Google AdSense requirements.

4. Google AdSense

We display ads via Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized ads at adssettings.google.com.

5. Third Party Services

File conversion is powered by CloudConvert. Files are transmitted securely to their servers for processing and deleted immediately after. Please refer to CloudConvert's privacy policy for more details.

6. Children's Privacy

FluxFiles is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at our contact page.
